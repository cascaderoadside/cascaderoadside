## Posting Schedule & Tips

### Frequency

- **Craigslist:** Post 1 variation every 48 hours (rotate A → B → C)
- **Nextdoor:** Post once at launch, then once per month with updates or special offers
- **Facebook:** 2-3 posts per week (mix of services, testimonials, tips, announcements)

### Best Times to Post

- **Craigslist:** Tuesday-Thursday, 10 AM - 2 PM (when people search)
- **Nextdoor:** Evening (6-8 PM) when neighbors are browsing
- **Facebook:** Weekday lunch time (12-1 PM) or evening (7-9 PM)

### Tips for Better Results

1. **Use local keywords** in your titles (Puyallup, Tacoma, Pierce County)
2. **Lead with price** — people want to know cost upfront
3. **Emphasize speed** — "30 minutes" is a competitive advantage
4. **Be honest** — "licensed & insured" builds trust
5. **Include phone number** — make it impossible to miss
6. **Rotate posts** — fresh posts get better visibility
7. **Respond fast** — if someone comments, reply within 1 hour

### What NOT to Do

- ❌ Use excessive emojis (2-3 max)
- ❌ Use ALL CAPS for entire sentences
- ❌ Promise unrealistic times ("5 minutes")
- ❌ Leave generic descriptions ("call for prices")
- ❌ Copy competitor language
- ❌ Post the same thing every day (Craigslist flags as spam)

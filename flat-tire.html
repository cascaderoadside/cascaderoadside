⚠️ Safety Tips for Flat Tires

Never Change a Tire on a Highway

Changing a tire near traffic is dangerous. Call us instead and stay safely in your vehicle.

Keep Hazard Lights On

Alert other drivers to your presence so they can slow down and give you space.

Spares Are Temporary

Don't drive long distances on a spare tire. Visit a repair shop within a few days to get your tire fixed.

Get Your Tire Inspected

A puncture or blowout can signal underinflated or worn tires. Have a professional inspect your vehicle.

Check Your Spare

Spares can go flat too. Periodically check your spare tire pressure at a tire shop or gas station.

Replace Worn Tires

Tires older than 6 years or with tread depth less than 2/32" are safety risks. Replace them.

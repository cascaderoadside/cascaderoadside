💡 Tips to Avoid Running Out of Gas

Watch Your Fuel Gauge

Fill up when your tank hits 1/4 full. Don't wait until the gas light comes on.

Plan Long Drives

On road trips, know where gas stations are. Plan stops every 100–150 miles.

Check for Fuel Leaks

If your fuel gauge drops suddenly, you may have a leak. Visit a mechanic.

Keep Gas Stations Nearby

Use your phone to map nearby stations before you get too low on fuel.

Avoid Old Fuel

Don't let your car sit for months without driving; old fuel can gum up your engine.

Use a Fuel Economy App

Apps like GasBuddy help you find nearby cheap gas stations.
